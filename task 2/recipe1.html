White Sauce Pasta

White sauce pasta, a delectable and creamy dish, combines al dente pasta with a velvety sauce crafted from butter, flour, and milk. The sauce's richness is further elevated by the addition of Parmesan cheese, delivering a harmonious balance of flavors. With a hint of black pepper for a touch of warmth, this classic Italian-inspired comfort food offers a comforting and indulgent culinary experience.

Cooking Time: 15min

Prepare Time: 30min

Instructions

Step 1: Boil the Pasta

1. Bring a pot of water to a rolling boil.

2. Add a pinch of salt and your desired quantity of pasta.

3. Cook the pasta until it's "al dente" (firm to the bite).

4. Drain the pasta in a colander and set aside.

Step 2: Prepare the Sauce

1. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.

2. Add an equal amount of all-purpose flour and whisk to form a smooth roux.

3. Gradually pour in milk while whisking to avoid lumps.

4. Keep stirring until the sauce thickens and becomes smooth.

Step 3: Add Cheese and Seasoning

1. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese until it melts into the sauce.

2. Season the sauce with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste.

Step 4: Combine Pasta and Sauce

1. Add the boiled pasta to the white sauce.

2. Gently toss the pasta to coat it evenly with the sauce.

Step 5: Serve and Enjoy

1. Transfer the creamy white sauce pasta to serving plates.

2. Optionally, sprinkle some additional Parmesan cheese and black pepper on top.

3. Serve the pasta hot and savor its delicious flavors.

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Tools

  • Hand Blender
  • Large Heavy Pot with Lid
  • Measuring Spoons
  • Measuring Cups

Quick Tips

  • Use Fresh Ingredients
  • Cook Pasta Al Dente
  • Whisk the Roux
  • Gradually Add Milk
  • Consider Extra Ingredients
  • Toss Pasta Gently
  • Use Grated Cheese
  • Garnish Creatively
  • Serve Immediately
