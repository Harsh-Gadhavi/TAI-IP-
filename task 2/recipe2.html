Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese, a classic Italian dish, features a hearty meat-based sauce served over cooked spaghetti pasta. The sauce is crafted by simmering ground meat (usually beef), aromatic vegetables, such as onions, carrots, and celery, in a rich tomato base. Flavored with herbs like basil and oregano, the sauce develops a robust, savory flavor profile. This comforting dish is a favorite worldwide, known for its satisfying combination of tender pasta and flavorful meat sauce, often topped with grated Parmesan cheese for an extra touch of indulgence.

Cooking Time: 30min

Prepare Time: 45min

Instructions

Step 1: Heat a large pot or pan over medium heat.

1. Add a drizzle of olive oil and sauté the chopped onion, carrots, and celery until they start to soften, about 5 minutes.

2. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another 1-2 minutes.

3. Cook the pasta until it's "al dente" (firm to the bite).

Step 2: Brown the Meat:

1. Push the vegetables to the side of the pan and add the ground beef.

2. Break up the meat with a spoon and cook until it's no longer pink and has browned.

Step 3: Add Tomatoes and Flavorings:

1. Stir in the tomato paste, dried oregano, and dried basil. Cook for 2 minutes to enhance the flavors.

2. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, beef broth, and red wine (if using). Mix well.

Step 4: Simmer the Sauce:

1. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for at least 1 to 2 hours, stirring occasionally. The longer it simmers, the richer the flavors will become.

Step 5: Cook the Pasta:

1. In a separate pot, bring water to a boil and add a pinch of salt.

2. Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente according to the package instructions.

3. Drain the pasta and set aside.

Step 6: Combine Pasta and Sauce:

1. When the sauce has thickened and developed a rich flavor, season it with salt and pepper to taste.

2. Combine the cooked pasta with the Bolognese sauce and toss to coat the pasta evenly.

Step 7: Serve:

1. Divide the Spaghetti Bolognese among serving plates.

2. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.

Step 8: Enjoy:

1. Serve the Spaghetti Bolognese hot and enjoy the hearty and flavorful dish!

Ingredients

  • 350g spaghetti
  • 300g ground meat (or a mix of meat and pork)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, peeled and finely chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup beef or vegetable broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)
