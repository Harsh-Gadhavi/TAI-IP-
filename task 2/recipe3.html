Chicken Pasta

Chicken pasta is a delightful and versatile dish that combines tender pieces of cooked chicken with cooked pasta, often accompanied by a flavorful sauce. The sauce can vary, ranging from creamy Alfredo to zesty tomato-based sauces. Vegetables, herbs, and seasonings are often added to enhance the dish's taste and nutritional value. Chicken pasta offers a balanced and satisfying meal, blending protein, carbohydrates, and savory flavors. It's a popular choice on menus worldwide due to its adaptability and the wide range of flavors it can offer. Whether creamy or tangy, chicken pasta remains a comfort food enjoyed by many.

Cooking Time: 30min

Prepare Time: 45min

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pasta:

1. Bring a pot of water to a boil

2. Add a pinch of salt and the pasta.

3. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente.

4. Drain the pasta and set aside.

Step 2: Cook the Chicken:

1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.

2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are browned and cooked through.

3. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.

Step 3: Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.

1. In the same pan, add a bit more olive oil if needed.

2. Sauté the chopped onion until it's translucent and slightly golden.

3. Add the minced garlic and sauté for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

Step 4: Add Tomatoes and Seasonings:

1. Stir in the diced tomatoes and let them cook for a few minutes.

2. Add the dried basil and oregano, and season with salt and pepper.

Step 5: Pour in Cream::

1. Pour in the heavy cream, stirring to combine.

2. Let the sauce simmer for a few minutes until it starts to thicken..

Step 6: Combine Chicken and Sauce:

1. Return the cooked chicken to the pan and stir it into the sauce

2. Let everything cook together for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

Step 7: Combine Pasta and Sauce:

1. Add the cooked pasta to the pan with the chicken and sauce.

2. Toss everything together to coat the pasta with the creamy sauce.

Step 8: Serve::

1. Divide the chicken pasta among serving plates.

Step 9: Garnish and Enjoy:

1. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh parsley leaves.

2. Serve the chicken pasta hot and savor the delicious flavors!

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (such as penne or fettuccine)
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (for serving)
  • Fresh parsley leaves (for garnish)
